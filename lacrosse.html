About Me & the Sport

Lacrosse is one of the oldest team sports in North America, originating with Indigenous peoples long before European contact. It combines the physicality of hockey, the field strategy of soccer, and the hand-eye coordination of baseball — making it one of the most demanding sports to master.

As a goalie, I am the last line of defense. The position demands split-second reaction times, vocal leadership, and an ability to reset mentally after giving up a goal — which separates elite goalies from average ones.

I've been playing since I was young and have dedicated thousands of hours to mastering the craft, both as a player and as a coach.

Coaching Philosophy

🧠

Mental Toughness

The best goalies forget a goal within seconds. I teach athletes to reset, refocus, and compete on the next shot regardless of what just happened.

⚡

Reaction Training

Goalie-specific drills designed to sharpen hand-eye coordination and cut reaction time — because a tenth of a second is the difference between a save and a goal.

🎯

Technical Precision

Footwork, stick positioning, angle play — every technical element is drilled until it becomes instinctual under game pressure.
